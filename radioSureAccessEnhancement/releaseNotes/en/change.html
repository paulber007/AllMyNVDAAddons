1. v2.1-dev (11/10/2020)

  • compatibility with NVDA 2020.3.
  • change of gestures "control + left and right arrow" by "alt + left and right arrow".
  • possibility of choosing the combination "shift+control" instead of "alt + control" for input gestures.
  • addition of the "What's new" button in the dialog proposing the installation of a new update.

2. v2.0 (07/16/2020)

  • compatibility with NVDA 2020.2
  • addition of scripts to activate the contextual menu of each column of the header of the station list.
  • configurable deactivation of progress bar tracking.

Adding TR translation
